About Us

    Raising the status of LGBTQ community in Kenya, through awareness and educational resources

    Improving LGBTQ community self-esteem, body image, and mental health

    Providing a safe space for persecuted members

    Providing psychosocial support to members who are victims of stereotypes, in whatever form that may be
